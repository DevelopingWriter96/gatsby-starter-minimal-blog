When Fox Mccloud gets a Distress call from the Mysterious Dinosaur Planet, he must team up with a stranger from an extinct race to help restore things to their former glory. However, as he does so he uncovers secrets about his own past that will change everything he knows about the life he lives and paves the way for a destiny in the fate of not one, but two worlds he never imagined.
